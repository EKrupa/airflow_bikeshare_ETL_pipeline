About Me

I’m Evan Krupa, a Python-focused Data Engineer and Web Developer with a passion for building scalable, data-driven systems and clean, user-centered applications.

My background bridges data engineering and web development, allowing me to work comfortably across the stack. I design and orchestrate ETL pipelines using Python, Apache Airflow, SQL, and deploy cloud-native solutions on AWS (S3, EC2). I enjoy building reliable, production-ready data workflows that support analytics and decision-making.

On the web side, I develop responsive applications with Django, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—focusing on clean architecture, readable code, and intuitive interfaces that support complex backend logic.

Outside of tech, I’m a musician who plays guitar and piano, and I enjoy riding my fixed gear bike. Music and cycling help shape how I approach engineering—disciplined, creative, and always improving.
